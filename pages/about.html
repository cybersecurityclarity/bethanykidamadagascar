About BethanyKids Madagascar

A sister initiative of BethanyKids — bringing life-saving pediatric surgery to the children of Madagascar.

Our Mission

Surgery Where It's Needed Most

BethanyKids Madagascar exists to close the devastating gap in pediatric surgical care in Madagascar. With only 5 pediatric surgeons for 22 million people, thousands of children are born with treatable conditions — cleft lips, spina bifida, clubfoot, hernias, and more — that go untreated for lack of access.

We believe every child deserves a chance at life, regardless of where they are born or how much their family earns. Our model is simple: send the surgeon to the patient, not the other way around.

Through partnerships with hospitals across Madagascar, we coordinate surgical outreach visits, fund operations for children who cannot pay, and build local surgical capacity for the future.

What We Do

🔪

Pediatric Surgery Outreach

Dr. Heuric travels to partner hospitals across Madagascar, performing life-changing surgeries for children who cannot reach the capital.

💰

Sponsored Surgeries

Donor support covers surgical costs for families who cannot afford to pay, ensuring no child is turned away for financial reasons.

🏥

Hospital Partnerships

We partner with hospitals willing to gather and prepare surgical cases, amplifying Dr. Heuric's reach across the island nation.

📚

Training Future Surgeons

Dr. Heuric mentors young Malagasy doctors, building local capacity that will outlast any individual surgeon's career.

🌍

Medical Exchange

We facilitate exchanges between international medical professionals and Malagasy doctors, enriching both sides with knowledge.

🙏

Faith-Centred Care

Rooted in Christian values of compassion and service, we bring both medical excellence and spiritual care to every patient.

Our Family

Part of the BethanyKids Network

BethanyKids Madagascar is a sister initiative of BethanyKids, one of Africa's leading organizations for training pediatric surgical teams. Since 2004, BethanyKids has provided life-transforming surgeries to over 60,000 children across Africa.

Dr. Heuric completed his pediatric surgery fellowship at BethanyKids' training site at Kijabe Hospital in Kenya, graduating in 2010. He returned to Madagascar committed to replicating that model of excellence and compassion in his home country.

BethanyKids Madagascar shares BethanyKids' mission, values, and commitment to Jesus-centred healthcare — and directs donations to support Dr. Heuric's outreach work on the ground.
