Children born with congenital conditions — cleft lips, spina bifida, clubfoot, hernias, hydrocephalus — face a death rate approximately 100× higher in Sub-Saharan Africa than in North America. Lack of access to a surgeon is the primary reason.

Knowledge Exchange

Teaching & Learning Together

BethanyKids Madagascar believes that the exchange of knowledge goes both ways. As Medical Director at Manambaro Hospital, Dr. Heuric has hosted Professor Dr. Richard Roach from the University of Western Michigan, along with multiple groups of medical residents and students.

In these exchanges, Malagasy doctors teach visiting students about tropical medicine, while visiting physicians provide updates on the latest treatments for conditions like diabetes, hypertension, and strokes.

These partnerships strengthen both sides — advancing Malagasy medical knowledge while giving international students hands-on experience in global health contexts.
