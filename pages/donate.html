The Why

Why Your Gift Matters

Children in Madagascar are born with surgical conditions — cleft lips, spina bifida, clubfoot, hernias — that are routinely corrected in the developed world. In Madagascar, without intervention, these children face lives of pain, disability, social exclusion, and often death.

Dr. Heuric travels across the country to bring surgery directly to these children. But every trip, every operation, requires resources — travel costs, surgical supplies, medications, follow-up care.

Your donation makes each surgery possible. It is that direct, and that meaningful.

"Every donor gives for different reasons, and one thing always seems true: it's not just the child getting surgery whose life is transformed."
— BethanyKids
