Meet the Surgeon

Dr. Heuric Rakotomalala

One of Madagascar's most skilled pediatric and general surgeons, Dr. Heuric has dedicated his career to serving children on the margins — traveling to hospitals across the country to bring surgical care where it is desperately needed.

Trained through PAACS (Pan African Academy of Christian Surgeons) and holding a pediatric surgery fellowship from BethanyKids at Kijabe Hospital, Kenya, he now serves as Medical Director at Manambaro Lutheran Hospital in southeastern Madagascar.

"This gentle doctor is the epitome of what everyone expects in a great doctor."
— Rita Gilmour Power, nominator

Our Model

How We Reach Children

Dr. Heuric travels to partner hospitals across Madagascar, operating on children who could not otherwise access surgery.

Hospital Partners Gather Cases

Partner hospitals identify children with surgical needs and schedule a visit from Dr. Heuric's team.

Dr. Heuric Travels to You

Dr. Heuric and his team travel to the hospital — bringing expertise directly to the patient, removing access barriers.

Surgery Is Performed

Children receive life-changing operations including cleft lip repair, spina bifida correction, VP shunts, and general pediatric surgery.

Donors Fund the Mission

Donations cover travel, surgical supplies, and follow-up care — making each surgery possible for families who cannot pay.
